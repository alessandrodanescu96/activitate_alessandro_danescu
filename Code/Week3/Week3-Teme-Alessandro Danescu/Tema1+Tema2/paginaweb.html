From Zero to Hero

After the Voice, I started to work on my own music, finally i was able to write the song Multumesc, made by Alexandru Anghel, Vlad Brancoveanu and Alessandro Danescu.

The idea of the song was inspired by daily thoughts, that we don't have time to be thankful for everything that you achieved. To be happy with the small things, to be proud of who we are and to be greatful.

Alessandro Dănescu (23 years old) is a student in Bucharest, at the Polytechnic, majoring in Aerospace Engineering. He discovered his musical talent in the fifth grade, when his music teacher told him he had a musical ear. He says he inherited his passion for music from his grandparents - his grandfather played the flute and his grandmother had a very beautiful voice.
